Rehearsal BBQ

There are no overnight accommodations for the rehearsal barbecue Friday night. If you would like to join us, and all are welcome, please plan to take the Fishers Island Ferry. We live close to the dock and you shouldn't have to take a car over on Friday.

Staying On-Island Saturday Night

Unfortunately, there are no hotel rooms for rent on Fishers Island. However, a few families in the community have graciously opened their homes to those wishing to stay on the island Saturday night. Please let us know as soon as possible so we can pair you with a host family as these opportunities are limited.

Please plan on taking the Fishers Island Ferry Saturday morning. Guests staying on the island are encouraged to carpool to reduce congestion on the ferry. If you are taking a car on the ferry, please make a reservation with the ferry for both your arrival on Saturday and departure on Sunday. Cars must be at the dock at least 25 minutes before the scheduled departure time or your reservation will be lost.

Cars not coming to the island can park at the Water Street Garage, just across the street from the ferry dock.

Off-Island Accommodations

We have reserved a block of rooms in the Hyatt in Mystic, CT. The hotel is only 15 minutes from Spicer's Marina in Noank. We have chartered the Popeye to take guests from Spicer’s to the island and back on Saturday. Since you cannot take your car across, transportation on the island will be provided.
